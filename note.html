About

Real-Time Fires with Hunt Areas

This application displays real-time fire information from MODIS Fire Detections and USGS Active Fire Perimeters alongside the most recent Fire Emergency Area Closures for Idaho from Idaho InciWeb Closures. The Fire Emergency Closures are for closure areas, not specific roads and trails. For details on road and trail closures visit the InciWeb Closures website.

Hunt boundaries for Controlled Hunts, Game Management Units and Elk Zones may be added by selecting a hunt from the left-hand menu. For more layers and options visit the Idaho Fish and Game Huntplanner MapCenter.

Background terrain maps, road maps and aerial imagery may be interchanged by toggling the map dropdown in the upper-right.

Data Sources

This application is constructed by "mashing up" publicly-available data sources from InciWeb, U.S. Forest Service, USGS, National Interagency Fire Center, Idaho Fish and Wildlife Information System, OpenStreetMap and Google Maps.

  • InciWeb Incident Information System Idaho Closures
  • ActiveFireMaps WMS Tiled Layer
  • USGS Active Geomac Perimeters Layer
  • National Interagency Fire Center FTP Server
  • Idaho Fish and Wildlife Information System Open Data - KML or SHP downloads
  • MapQuest OpenStreetMap Tile Service
  • Google Maps Javascript API V3

GIS Data Downloads

  • Real-Time MODIS Fire Detections Layer (kml, shp, wms)
  • Fire Closures maintained by IDFG, Boise NF and wildfire incident management teams. (ArcGIS Map Server, Google Earth Layer)
